Zeroocho Records is an electronic music label based in Barcelona. At the begining, it was created with the purpose to collaborate with locals artists and be a platform to push these talents.
After a stop, we are starting a new stage, eager to do more collaborations, release more music and share our vision.

Cosmic House EP

Cosmic House es el debut en formato EP del productor afincado en Barcelona Inner Axes. El release verá la luz el próximo 24 de septiembre a través de su sello Zeroocho Records y contará con una remezcla del productor Berlinés Benedikt Frey.

Premiere: Inner Axes - Cosmic House (Benedikt Frey Hybrid Megamix)

Zeroocho Records next EP comes via Inner Axes: the aptly named 'Cosmic House' which draws from his love of Italo, 90s house, acid and jungle On the flip we're treated to a powerful trance-tinged reinterpretation of the title track from Serious Trouble co-founder Benedikt Frey.

44,100Hz Social Club Premiere: Inner Axes - Italo Cósmico (Original Mix)

Italo Cósmico is a track that evokes the 80's. A combination of RETRO—FUTURISTE sounds, keys and female vocals inspired by the House Music.

Whypeopledance Premiere: Cosmic House (Original Mix)

COSMIC HOUSE is the first single of INNER AXES upcoming EP. It is a HOUSE MUSIC track that leads the listener into the summertime and the open-air dance parties. The song evolves from the 90’s HOUSE and JUNGLE music inspiration with snare rolls and breaks. A touch of ACID and COSMIC sounds culminate the composition of this club weapon track.

DHA Premiere: INNER AXES FEAT. JUANJO PEDRO – TASTE OF GOD (TIMOTHY CLERKIN REMIX)

The Amsterdam producer parts from the deeper pad and acid sound found on the original track and takes it from there into a place close to progressive house, with epic melodies and steamy sounds, a mixture that evokes nocturnally and dance.

44,100Hz Social Club PREMIERE | Inner Axes Feat. Juanjo Pedro - Taste Of God (IWM Remix)

Barcelona producer Inner Axes is about to release his new single "Taste of God" in a collaboration with Juanjo Pedro plus remixes from Timothy Clerkin and Indies With Microkorges (IWM) - Madrid based duo consisting of JackWasFaster and Johnston Jonhston who takes acid sound found on the original version and create an enigmatic slow paced techno track, which highlights snare drums rhythms, percussive sounds and glitches.

whypeopledance PRÈMIÉRE | Inner Axes - Taste Of God (ft. Juanjo Pedro)

Taste of God" is one song of Inner Axes’s upcoming EP. For this release, the Barcelona based producer counts with the collaborations of Juanjo Pedro on the vocals, TimothyClerkin and Indies With Microkorgs (IWM) on the remixes. The first track is the original version, in which Juanjo Pedro ́s voice has a great prominence. Sounds of Sónar by Ghostly Enemies

Between Seattle and Barcelona, electronic duo Ghostly Enemies is built on duality. Producer Steven Sanchez and vocalist Juanjo Pedro formed over a common goal: to create dark ambient music with pop accessibility. It is a combination of both of their backgrounds, highlighting Steven’s dark production and Juanjo’s affecting vocals. A lineal magnetism where you can intuit their influences from Trip Hop, Dark Synth Pop and Downtempo.
For the series "Sounds of Sónar", producer Steven Sánchez has prepared a Dj set thinking in the dancefloor to celebrate their upcoming actuation in Sónar Festival.
